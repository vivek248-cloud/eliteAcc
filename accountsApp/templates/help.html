Visual Indicators & Status
STATUS MARKER CONTEXT & MEANING
Green / Credit Positive liquidity, Received Amount, or profitable balance.
Red / Debit Debited Amount, expenditure, or amount yet to be collected.
Warning Overpayment detected: Client paid more than the budget or spending exceeded expectations.
System Info Shows historical data: Previously spent or Previously paid records.
⚠ Bank Alert Negative Bank Balance: Occurs when spending happens before client payment. Resolves on credit.
Accounting Formulas
Total Paid Cheque + Cash

Total liquidity received from the client across all modes.

Net Balance Total Paid − Total Spent

The actual available remaining funds for a specific client/project.

Yet To Pay Project Budget − Total Paid

Remaining amount the client owes based on the agreed project value.

General Usage Rules
  • Only numeric cells are selectable for the auto-sum feature.
  • PDF Exports are dynamic; they always respect your currently applied filters.
  • Bank opening balances should ideally be zero during initial setup to maintain ledger integrity.
  • Note: Set zero opening balances for all bank accounts during creation.
  • Note: Please ensure cash bank selected for cash payment mode
Spending & Liquidity
  • Always verify a Positive Bank Balance before finalizing new expenditure entries.
  • The system performs an automatic deduction from the linked bank ledger upon creation.
  • Updating a spending record will re-calculate and adjust the bank balance in real-time.
  • Removing an entry will instantly restore the funds to the corresponding bank account.
